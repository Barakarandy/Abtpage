Who We Are

Unyenyekevu Ministry is a Christian-based, non-profit Community-Based Organization (CBO) founded in 2014 in Kenya, serving vulnerable communities in Kakuma Refugee Camp (Turkana County) and Kitale. Rooted in compassion and humility.

Unyenyekevu works to transform lives by addressing the interconnected needs of underprivileged children, orphans, youths, and persons living with disabilities through a holistic approach that integrates education, healthcare, psycho-social support, economic empowerment, and spiritual growth.
